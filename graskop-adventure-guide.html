The Adventurer's Guide to Graskop

Beyond the viewpoints lies Mpumalanga's adrenaline capital. Discover the thrilling gorge swings, ziplines, and hikes that make Graskop a hub for adventure.

While Graskop is famous as the gateway to the Panorama Route's scenic wonders, it has also carved out a reputation as a playground for thrill-seekers. The dramatic landscape of towering cliffs, deep gorges, and lush forests provides the perfect setting for a wide range of heart-pumping activities.

If your idea of a holiday involves more adrenaline than quiet contemplation, this guide is for you. Here are the top adventures to be had in and around Graskop.

The Graskop Gorge Lift Co: A Hub of Adventure

This world-class facility is the epicenter of Graskop's adventure scene. Itâ€™s built on the edge of the Graskop Gorge and offers several incredible experiences in one location.

The Viewing Lift & Forest Boardwalk

The main attraction is the viewing lift that travels 51 meters down the cliff face into the Afromontane forest below. The journey itself offers spectacular views. At the bottom, a 600-meter network of elevated boardwalks, suspension bridges, and interactive exhibits winds through the pristine forest, offering a unique perspective of the ecosystem and the Motitsi waterfall.

The Big Swing & Zipline

For the ultimate adrenaline rush, look no further. The Big Swing is one of the world's highest cable gorge swings. You leap off the top of a waterfall, plunging into a 70-meter freefall before swinging across the gorge. It's an unforgettable, scream-inducing experience for true adrenaline junkies.

If the Big Swing is a step too far, the 130-meter high-wire zipline offers a thrilling but less terrifying way to experience the gorge from above. You'll glide across the gorge with incredible views of the landscape below.

  • Location: Graskop Gorge Lift Co, just outside Graskop on the R533.
  • Visitor Info: You can buy tickets for each activity separately or as a combo. Booking in advance, especially for the Big Swing, is highly recommended during peak seasons.

Hiking: Explore the Escarpment on Foot

Graskop is a fantastic base for hikers. The surrounding area is crisscrossed with trails that take you deep into the Blyde River Canyon Nature Reserve and through vast forestry plantations. Trails vary in difficulty, from gentle walks to strenuous multi-day treks.

Popular Hiking Trail: The Leopard Trail

Also known as the Oorlogskloof Trail, this is a challenging but rewarding hike that starts near the Blyde River. It offers some of the most spectacular scenery in the canyon. Shorter sections can be done as day walks, but proper planning, permits, and a good level of fitness are essential. Always check with local authorities or hiking clubs for the latest trail conditions and safety advice.

Post-Adventure Refuel: The Pancake Capital
Graskop is famous for its pancake restaurants. After a day of swinging and zipping, there's no better reward than a delicious pancake. Head to legendary spots like Harrie's Pancakes or The Glass House for a huge selection of sweet and savoury options. It's a Graskop tradition!

Essential Adventure Tips

To make the most of your adventure in Graskop, keep these tips in mind.
  • Book in Advance: High-demand activities like the Big Swing can get fully booked. Secure your spot online before you go.
  • Wear Appropriate Gear: For any adventure activity, closed-toe shoes with good grip are essential. Wear comfortable clothing that you can move in easily.
  • Trust the Professionals: Only use reputable, accredited operators for activities like hiking, abseiling, or canyoning. Safety should always be the number one priority.
  • Check the Weather: Mist and rain can sometimes lead to the temporary closure of activities. It's always a good idea to check the forecast and call ahead.
